About

This project was inspired by a personal passion for mazes and puzzle-solving. It all started when I was a child and encountered my first maze book. The thrill of finding the correct path and solving complex puzzles ignited my curiosity and sparked a lifelong fascination with mazes.

This project is a part of my portfolio for Holberton School, where I am honing my skills in software development. You can learn more about Holberton School and their programs here.
